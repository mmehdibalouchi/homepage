Allame Helli IV High School (2010 - 2014)

Introduction

Allame Helli 4 is one of the best NODET (National Organization for Development of Exceptional Talents) schools in Tehran that means you need to pass two difficult exams to get to this kind of schools!

My Total score was 19.83/20 and I ranked no.1 student for 4 years individually and in total!

My Passion

I was really into computers even before high school; they were magical and complex and that was the reason I loved them, but high school was the most important period of my educational life, my first interact with programming, my first line of code and it began! I believe that high school had a big role in my further decisions about my career and even my favourite field of science. I also joined the physics Olympiad team in high school because I'm deeply into mechanics and optics!
